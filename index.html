Resistance Bootcamp 2.0 is a two-day intensive training for organizers and activists, organized by the Portland Metro Indivisible Network and co-sponsors. The bootcamp emphasizes movement resilience and sustainability through affinity groups, leadership development, and conflict resolution frameworks.


Featuring sixteen interactive hands-on sessions, the curriculum serves both newcomers and experienced advocates with practical tools for grassroots organizing. Trainers focus on peer education through teach-back exercises, aiming to expand training throughout Oregon.
