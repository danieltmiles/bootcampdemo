Portland Metro Indivisible Network, together with its co-sponsoring groups, is convening a two-day intensive training for organizers and activists. This event will build on the foundations laid by Resistance Bootcamp 2025 to emphasize movement resilience and sustainability through the creation of affinity groups, development of leadership pipelines to avoid burnout, and establishing frameworks for conflict resolution within our groups and networks.

Two Days.

Sixteen Sessions.

Two Hundred Activists.

One Mass Movement of Nonviolent Resistance

Join us in Portland again this spring for two days of hands-on activist training. This event will offer timely instruction to empower grassroots activists and community organizers with the skills they need to build more sustainable local groups while also helping develop a more resilient movement. From a workshop on starting affinity groups to another on broadening leadership circles, every session aims to be more interactive and allow ample time for teach-back exercises to better equip participants to return to their groups as confident and effective peer educators. In this way, we hope to leverage the Bootcamp training experience as we expand training efforts throughout Oregon.

Whether you're new to activism or a seasoned advocate, the Resistance Bootcamp curriculum is designed to build solidarity and strengthen our collective impact. Learn from experienced trainers, connect with fellow activists, and discover practical tools for making change safely and effectively.
